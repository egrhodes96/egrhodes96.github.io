Student Outcomes

My goal is that students leave class thinking, “I can do science.” These are the outcomes I design for.

Scientific thinking

Students form testable questions, plan investigations, and revise explanations with evidence.

Lab confidence

Students learn safe, repeatable lab routines—measurement, data tables, analysis, and clear conclusions.

Wonder + rigor

High engagement through real organisms, field contexts, and phenomena—without sacrificing precision.
